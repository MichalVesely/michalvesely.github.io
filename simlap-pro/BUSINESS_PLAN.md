# SimLap Pro - Business Plan

## Executive Summary

SimLap Pro is a SaaS platform providing AI-powered telemetry analysis for sim racers. The platform helps users improve lap times through automated analysis and coaching insights.

**Target Revenue**: $1,000-2,000/month within 6 months
**Target Market**: 10M+ sim racing enthusiasts worldwide
**Business Model**: Freemium subscription service
**Competitive Advantage**: 50% cheaper than alternatives with AI-powered insights

---

## Market Analysis

### Market Size
- **Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: 10M+ sim racers globally
- **Serviceable Available Market (SAM)**: 2M competitive sim racers
- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)**: 50,000 serious racers willing to pay

### Target Customer
**Primary**: Competitive sim racers (18-45 years old)
- Actively participate in online racing
- Own sim racing equipment ($500-5000)
- Subscribe to racing services (iRacing, etc.)
- Seek performance improvement
- Disposable income for hobbies

**Secondary**: Racing teams and leagues
- Need telemetry analysis tools
- Manage multiple drivers
- Willing to pay for team licenses

### Competitor Analysis

| Competitor | Price | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|------------|-------|-----------|------------|
| Track Titan | $20-30/mo | Established, Feature-rich | Expensive, Complex |
| Sim Racing Telemetry | $20/mo | Multi-platform | No AI insights |
| MoTeC i2 | One-time $150 | Professional tool | Steep learning curve |
| Coach Dave Delta | $15-25/mo | Good community | Limited analysis depth |

**Our Position**: Premium features at budget pricing with AI assistance

## Cost Structure

### Fixed Costs (Monthly)
- Server hosting (DigitalOcean): $12
- Domain & SSL: $2
- Stripe fees (2.9% + $0.30): ~$35 at 100 customers
- **Total Fixed**: ~$50/month

### Variable Costs (Per User)
- OpenAI API (optional): $0.01-0.05 per analysis
- Storage: Negligible
- Bandwidth: Included in hosting

### Break-Even Analysis
- Break-even: 5 paying customers ($50/month)
- Target ($1000/month): 100 paying customers
- Nice profit ($2000/month): 200 paying customers

**Gross Margin**: ~95% (SaaS typical)
**Net Profit at 100 customers**: ~$950/month

## Marketing Strategy

### Phase 1: Community Building (Month 1-3)
**Budget**: $0-100/month (mostly time)

**Tactics**:
1. **Reddit Marketing** (2 hours/week):
   - Post in r/simracing, r/iRacing, r/ACCompetizione
   - Share success stories
   - Offer free analyses for feedback

2. **Discord Engagement** (1 hour/week):
   - Join sim racing communities
   - Help users, share insights
   - Offer free trials

3. **Content Creation** (3 hours/week):
   - Blog posts: "How to analyze telemetry"
   - Tutorial videos
   - Telemetry tips on social media

**Goal**: 10-25 paying customers, establish presence

### Phase 2: Growth (Month 4-6)
**Budget**: $100-300/month

**Tactics**:
1. **Influencer Partnerships**:
   - Reach out to sim racing YouTubers
   - Offer affiliate program (20% commission)
   - Sponsor videos ($50-200/video)

2. **Paid Advertising**:
   - Reddit ads targeting sim racing subreddits
   - Google Ads for "sim racing telemetry"
   - Facebook ads targeting sim racing interests

3. **SEO**:
   - Target long-tail keywords
   - Create comparison pages
   - Build backlinks from racing communities

**Goal**: 50-100 paying customers

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 7-12)
**Budget**: $300-500/month

**Tactics**:
1. **Paid Ads at Scale**:
   - Optimize performing campaigns
   - Expand to YouTube ads
   - Retargeting campaigns

2. **Partnership Program**:
   - League partnerships (bulk licenses)
   - Sim racing team sponsorships
   - Integration with racing platforms

3. **Retention Marketing**:
   - Email campaigns
   - Feature announcements
   - User success stories
   - Referral program

**Goal**: 150-300 paying customers
